Petroleum & Drilling Waste

Heavy Handling

Disposal of Tank Bottom Sludges & Drilling Waste

Drilling operations, oil depots, and refineries produce heavy, petroleum-rich sludges that are challenging to move and process. Our specialized team handles high-volume tank cleaning and safe disposal of oily wastes according to world-class standards.

Common Petroleum Waste Categories

Tank Bottom Sludge

Heavy organic residues from crude oil storage that require mechanical and chemical evacuation.

Drilling Mud

Chemical fluids used in drilling operations that must be dewatered and neutralized.

Oily Sludges

Hazardous mixtures of oil, water, and soil from refinery separators and infrastructure.

Certified Tank Cleaning

We specialized in hazardous tank cleaning and sludge recovery. Our team uses GPCB-approved methods to ensure zero-spill operations and provide the necessary disposal certificates for your environmental audits.
