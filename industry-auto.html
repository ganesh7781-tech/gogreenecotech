Automotive manufacturing generates a diverse range of hazardous wastes, spanning from paint shop sludges to waste oils and coolants. Efficient production lines require a waste partner that can scale with high-volume output while maintaining strict safety standards.

Common Automotive Waste Types

Paint Shop Sludge

Solvent-based or water-based paint residues that require specialized chemical treatment.

Used Oils & Coolants

Contaminated lubricants from machining and engine testing that need recovery or incineration.

ETP Sludge

Filter cakes from plant wastewater treatment containing heavy metals and grease.
