Homogenization & Blending

Waste Transformation

Creating High-Value Alternative Fuels (AFRM)

Our industrial blenders are the heart of the co-processing preparation cycle. We mix diverse solid and liquid hazardous wastes to create a homogenized mixture with a consistent calorific value, specifically engineered for high-temperature destruction in cement kilns.

Calorific Management: Precisely blended to maintain thermal efficiency for industrial kilns.
Vapor Control: Specialized systems to manage and neutralize VOCs during mixing.
Batch Stability: Rigorous homogenization ensures zero stratification in liquid-solid mixtures.

The Science of Blending

Industrial waste is often unpredictable. Our homogenization process eliminates variability, transforming difficult wastes into a stable "Alternative Fuel and Raw Material" (AFRM). This ensures that cement manufacturers receive a dependable resource, supporting the circular economy and reducing reliance on coal.
