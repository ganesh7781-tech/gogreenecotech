Co-Processing Edge

Supreme Technology

Total Destruction at 1400°C for Zero Waste

Co-processing is the ultimate solution for complex industrial wastes that cannot be recycled. By utilizing waste as an alternative fuel or raw material in cement kilns, we achieve total thermal destruction of hazardous molecules while recovering energy and mineral value.

Why Co-Processing?

Highest Temperatures

Operation at >1400°C ensures complete destruction of even the most stable hazardous compounds with no residue.

Energy Recovery

Waste thermal energy replaces fossil fuels like coal, significantly reducing the carbon footprint of cement production.

No Land-fill

Unlike incineration, co-processing leaves no ash or residue behind, providing a true 'Zero Waste' lifecycle.

Pre-Processing Excellence

Before co-processing, we shred and homogenize diverse waste streams into a uniform 'Alternative Fuel and Raw Material' (AFRM). Our Boisar facility is equipped with high-torque shredders and massive blending bunkers to prepare waste for supreme destruction.
