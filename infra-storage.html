CPCB-Authorized Secure Storage Facilities

Safety begins with secure containment. Our Boisar facility offers multi-layered, specialized storage for all categories of hazardous industrial materials. Designed with over 15 years of industry experience, our storage area ensures total zero-leakage and zero-contamination.

Fire Suppression: Integrated hydrant and CO2 systems specialized for chemical spills.
Secondary Containment: Impermeable dyke walls to contain 110% of total stored liquid volume.
Segregation Protocols: Matrix-based color coding to prevent mixing of incompatible chemicals.
