Mitigating Fire Hazards in Solvents & Pigments

Production high fire risks are mitigated by our vacuum systems for safety. We process waste results from the mechanical mixing of pigments and solvents without chemical reactions, focusing on secure containment and volume reduction.

Waste Stream Breakdown

Solvent Slops

Highly flammable mixtures from equipment cleaning that require explosion-proof handling.

Pigment Blocks

Solidified ink and varnish residues that must be shredded and co-processed safely.

Expired Inks

Bulk quantities of off-spec or expired printing inks and varnishes requiring certified destruction.

Vacuum-Based Extraction

We use specialized vacuum systems to evacuate paint and ink containers, reducing the risk of solvent exposure and fires during the waste handling process. Our facilities are designed to handle bulk flammable wastes with zero incidents.
