FMCG Supply Chain

Zero Waste Path

Solving Complex Waste in High-Volume Consumer Goods

Fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) produce complex waste streams from harsh chemical compositions in cleaning products to massive quantities of multi-layer plastic packaging. We provide a sustainable path for FMCG facilities to reach their ambitious zero-waste-to-landfill manufacturing goals.

Common FMCG Waste Categories

Off-Spec Products

Expired or defective cleaning agents, shampoos, and consumables requiring certified disposal.

Non-Recyclable Plastics

Multi-layered packaging (MLP) and laminated pouches that must be directed to co-processing.

Distribution Centers

Bulk quantities of damaged inventory and secondary packaging from the distribution supply chain.

Certified Brand Protection

We ensure that all off-spec or returns are destroyed securely to prevent counterfeit products from entering the gray market. Our team provides detailed certificates of destruction to protect your brand's integrity and value.
