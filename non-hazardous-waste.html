Non-Hazardous waste

Just because it isn’t toxic doesn’t mean it belongs in a landfill. We help factories turn their industrial byproducts back into valuable resources.

Putting Waste Back to Use

Industrial leftovers like Metals, Glass, and Plastics aren't just trash—they’re raw materials waiting for a second life. We set up professional sorting systems to capture these resources before they ever reach a landfill.

Core Materials:

Expert processing of industrial Metals, Glass, Plastics, and Paper products.

The Co-Processing Edge

Our pre-processing leads to co-processing in cement kilns at temperatures exceeding 1400°C. This ensures complete thermal destruction of organic components and a minimum gas retention of 6 seconds, providing a zero-land-disposal solution.

Technical Advantage:

High temperature (>1400°C) and long retention time (>6 sec) for total destruction.

Integrated Waste Solutions (IWS)

We offer total site waste management for complicated industrial layouts. By integrating both hazardous and non-hazardous collection, we provide a single point of responsibility for all your environmental compliance needs.

Unified Management:

One partner for hazardous disposal and resource recovery operations.
