About Go Green

We're Cleaning Up Industry

Go Green Eco Tech is more than just a waste company. We are a team of environmentalists with 15 years of practical experience helping Indian industries manage their hazardous and non hazardous byproducts responsibly and legally.

Our Mission

To provide practical, ethical, and affordable waste management that keeps your facility safe and your business in total compliance with CPCB regulations.

Our Vision

To build a circular economy where "waste" is no longer a problem, but a resource that fuels the next generation of Indian manufacturing.

  • Real Impact - We don't just move trash we process it using methods that actually protect our local ecosystems.
  • Legal Security - Sleep better knowing your waste is handled by ISO 14001 and CPCB certified experts.
